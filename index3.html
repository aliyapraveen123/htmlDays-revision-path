Introduction

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the capability of computational systems to perform tasks typically associated with human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and decision-making. It is a field of research in engineering, mathematics and computer science that develops and studies methods and software that enable machines to perceive their environment and use learning and intelligence to take actions that maximize their chances of achieving defined goals. 1

Advantages & Disadvantages

        Advantages

Automation:
AI can perform repetitive tasks automatically, reducing human effort and saving time.
High Accuracy:
AI can analyze large amounts of data with fewer errors than humans when properly trained.
24/7 Availability:
AI systems can work continuously without taking breaks or getting tired.
Fast Decision-Making:
AI processes information quickly and provides faster results in many situations.

       Disadvantages

Job Displacement:
Automation may replace some human jobs, especially repetitive ones.
High Development Cost:
Building and maintaining AI systems can be expensive.
Lack of Human Emotions:
AI cannot understand emotions or make decisions based on empathy like humans.
Privacy Concerns:
AI often requires large amounts of personal data, which may raise privacy issues.
